Vergil

Our Mission

Vergil is a student conceived initiative with goal of changing how we interact with the cultural artifacts that compose our collective heritage. Vergil is a network that connects undergraduates trained in art history with consumers interested in a better museum experience. Using Vergil, museum-goers can expect on-demand, quality, private and affordable guided tours of selected collections. In turn, students can expect to be paid for meaningfully engaging with actual works of art. Vergil is committed to developing its student guides to become professional interpreters of art and art historical criticism for the lay-public. Furthermore, experienced guides will be given opportunities to design, oversee and profit from the implementation of new tours.
